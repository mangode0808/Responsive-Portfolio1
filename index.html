About Me

I am an immigrant from Mongolia and I have been in USA for 11 years. I came in America to explore different race and learn interesting stories of America. However, I fell in love with the country. So I decided to stay here.I think I made a right choice. Building new life in someone else's country was not so easy at all, but I did. Now I live with my beautiful wife and adorable daughter. I really love to spend time with my family when I am free.I always think that if I try hard enough, I will get anything I wish to have.I always liked physical sports like basketball, ultimate fighting, and swimmiing... etc.
